FILM]

[ROOM 004

PHOTO COMPOSITION]

THiS PIECE REFLECTS ON THE IDEA THAT RELIGIOUS BELIEF IS ROOTED IN THE STRUCTURE AND FUNCTION OF THE HUMAN BRAIN. RATHER THAN UNDERSTANDING FAITH AS SOMETHING EXTERNAL OR DIVINE, IT PROPOSES THAT SPIRITUAL EXPERIENCES EMERGE FROM COGNITIVE PROCESSES SHAPED BY EVOLUTION. IT QUUESTIONS THE NATURE OF BELIEF, SUGGESTING THAT WHAT WE PERCEIVE AS TRANSCENDENCE MAY BE THE RESULT OF NEURAL ACTIVITY AND THE BRAIN´S NEED TO CREATE MEANING.

THIS PIECE EXPLORES THE MORAL AND STRUCTURAL IMBALANCE OF MODERN FINANCIAL SYSTEMS. IT REFELCTS A WORLD DRIVEN BY PROFIT, WHERE RISK, EXCESS AND SPECULATION REPLACE STABILITY AND ETHICAL RESPONSABILITY. INSPIRED BY THE DYNAMICS OF WALL STREET, THE WORK CRITIQUES A SYSTEM IN WHICH ECONOMIC POWER OPERATES WITH LITTLE ACCOUNTABILITY, WHILE ITS CONSEQUENCES EXTEND FAR BEYOND THOSE WHO CONTROL IT.
